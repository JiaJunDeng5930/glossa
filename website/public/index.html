The shape
of attention

What changes when a reader gives a sentence enough time?

Deep reading begins when 注意力 attention settles. A patient reader notices the 细微 subtle architecture of a sentence, follows an 陌生的 unfamiliar idea, and keeps moving until the whole argument becomes 清晰 clear .

THE READER · ESSAY

To read is to
remain with an idea.

A difficult paragraph often becomes clear when attention remains 稳定 steady . The reader begins to notice the 结构 structure beneath each claim, the 细微差别 nuance inside a phrase, and the words that become 不可或缺 indispensable to the argument.

Meaning accumulates quietly. One sentence prepares the next, and the page keeps its rhythm.
